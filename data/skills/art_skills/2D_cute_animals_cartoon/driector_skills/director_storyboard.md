# 分镜提示词 · 2D 卡通可爱动物 · 风格专属技法

---

## 适用范围

本 Skill 专用于**可爱动物**题材的分镜提示词生成：强调表情清晰、动作简洁、氛围温暖明亮。

---

## 情绪 → 表情/动作词映射

| 情绪输入 | 表情词 | 动作词 |
|---|---|---|
| 快乐/兴奋 | big smile, sparkling eyes | jumping, waving, tail wagging |
| 害羞/温柔 | shy smile, blushing cheeks | holding paws together, small wave |
| 惊讶/好奇 | wide eyes, small "o" mouth | leaning forward, tilting head |
| 困倦/安心 | sleepy eyes, gentle smile | yawning, curling up |
| 失落/委屈（轻度） | teary eyes (subtle), downturned mouth | sitting quietly, slow steps |

---

## 场景氛围词库（绘本向）

| 时间段 | 氛围词 |
|---|---|
| 清晨 | soft morning light, fresh air, gentle breeze |
| 午后 | warm sunlight, cozy, playful |
| 黄昏 | golden light, warm and calm |
| 夜晚室内 | warm lamp light, cozy room, bedtime mood |

---

## 固定风格锚定词（所有输出必须包含）

2D cartoon illustration, cute animals, clean line art,
soft pastel colors, storybook style, simple shading, soft shadow,
warm and bright, high resolution, crisp lines,
no text, no watermark
