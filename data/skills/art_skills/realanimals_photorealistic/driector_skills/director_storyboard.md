# 分镜提示词 · 写实动物摄影 · 风格专属技法

---

## 适用范围

本 Skill 专用于**写实动物纪录片/自然摄影**题材的分镜提示词生成：强调真实行为、自然光、镜头语言与对焦规则。

---

## 行为词库（真实动物）

| 行为 | 关键词 |
|---|---|
| 警觉观察 | alert, ears perked, scanning surroundings |
| 觅食 | foraging, grazing, hunting, pecking |
| 行走/奔跑 | walking, trotting, sprinting |
| 梳理毛发 | grooming, shaking off water |
| 亲子/群体 | feeding young, herd movement, social interaction |

---

## 光线与时间段

| 时间段 | 关键词 |
|---|---|
| 清晨 | soft morning light, cool tones |
| 午后 | neutral daylight, clear visibility |
| 黄昏 | golden hour, warm rim light |
| 阴天 | overcast soft light, muted colors |
| 雨雪 | light rain, snowflakes, mist |

---

## 固定风格锚定词（所有输出必须包含）

photorealistic wildlife documentary, wildlife photography,
natural light, realistic anatomy, detailed fur/feathers,
tack sharp eyes, realistic colors,
telephoto lens, shallow depth of field, bokeh,
high detail, high resolution,
no illustration, no cartoon, no CGI,
no text, no watermark
